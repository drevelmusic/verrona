For Promoters & Organizers

About the band

VERRONA is raw energy, heavy riffs and drive that stands the test of time. The band has a full-length English-language release on European label Alster Records and 8 successful Russian-language singles distributed through OneRPM and "Davay"

The band is now fully focused on the Russian-speaking audience and ready for active touring in Moscow and across the country. Band members are 25–45 years old. Professional approach and punctuality guaranteed.

Technical Requirements (brief)

  • Lineup: 4 members
  • Sound: Quality front-of-house required
  • Set duration: 45 - 60 minutes
